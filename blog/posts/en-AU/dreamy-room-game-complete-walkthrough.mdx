# Dreamy Room Complete Guide: Create a Cozy and Healing Living Space

Dreamy Room is a cozy and healing organization game developed by ABI Games Studio. Through organizing and decorating rooms, players can create unique living spaces while experiencing a heartwarming story.

## I. Basic Gameplay

### 1. Core Mechanics

- **Organization**: Find the perfect spot for each item
- **Space Design**: Create unique and cozy rooms
- **Story Experience**: Tell life stories through item placement
- **Level Progression**: Unlock more rooms and items gradually
- **Free Creation**: No time limits, express your creativity freely

### 2. Game Features

- **Stress Relief**: Perfect blend of mindfulness and creativity, away from daily hustle
- **Heartwarming Story**: Each item tells a life story
- **Comfortable Atmosphere**: Soft visuals and music, pressure-free experience
- **Organization Joy**: Create peace-inducing spaces
- **Emotional Resonance**: Evoke shared memories and feelings
- **Unique Gameplay**: Simple, intuitive, yet charming

### 3. Room System

- **More Spaces**: Unlock as you progress
