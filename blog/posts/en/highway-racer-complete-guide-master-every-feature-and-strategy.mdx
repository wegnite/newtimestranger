## Getting Started

Before you hit the road, it’s important to familiarize yourself with the basics. Here’s how to get started:

### Controls
Mastering the controls is the first step to success in **Highway Racer**. Here’s a quick breakdown:
- **Accelerate:** Press **W** or the **Up Arrow** key.
- **Move Left:** Press **A** or the **Left Arrow** key.
- **Move Right:** Press **D** or the **Right Arrow** key.
- **Brake:** Press **S** or the **Down Arrow** key.

These controls are intuitive and responsive, making it easy to weave through traffic.

### Game Modes
**Highway Racer** offers several modes to keep things exciting:
- **One Way:** Traffic flows in a single direction. Perfect for beginners!
- **Two Way:** Face oncoming traffic for an added challenge.
- **Time Attack:** Race against the clock to set the best time.
- **Bomb Mode:** Keep your speed up or risk exploding!

## Core Gameplay Mechanics

Understanding the core mechanics of **Highway Racer** is key to improving your performance. Here’s what you need to know:

### Traffic Navigation
The primary challenge in **Highway Racer** is avoiding traffic. Pay attention to the flow of cars and plan your moves ahead of time. Sudden lane changes can lead to crashes, so stay alert!

### Speed Management
While speed is essential, it’s not always your best friend. In Bomb Mode, for example, maintaining a high speed is crucial. However, in other modes, braking at the right time can save you from a collision.

### Car Selection
As you progress, you’ll unlock new cars with unique performance characteristics. Some cars are faster, while others handle better. Experiment with different vehicles to find the one that suits your playstyle.
