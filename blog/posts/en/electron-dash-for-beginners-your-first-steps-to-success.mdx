## Basic Controls and Interface

**Electron Dash** features intuitive controls that are easy to learn but challenging to master. Here's a breakdown of the basic controls:

- **Left and Right Arrow Keys**: Move your astronaut side to side to avoid obstacles.
- **Up Arrow Key or Spacebar**: Jump over obstacles and gaps in the tunnel.

Your astronaut runs automatically, so your primary focus is on navigating the tunnel and avoiding hazards. The interface is minimalistic, with your score displayed at the top of the screen and your lives represented by hearts. Collecting hearts will give you extra lives, allowing you to continue your run even if you make a mistake.

Now that you know the basics, it's time to take your first steps in **Electron Dash**. Here's a step-by-step walkthrough of your initial run:

1. **Start Running**: As soon as the game begins, your astronaut will start running automatically.
2. **Navigate the Tunnel**: Use the left and right arrow keys to move your astronaut side to side, avoiding obstacles.

## Next Steps

Once you've mastered the basics, it's time to take your skills to the next level. Here are some tips and strategies to help you achieve a high score:

1. **Stay Centered**: Try to keep your astronaut in the center of the tunnel as much as possible. This gives you more time to react to obstacles on either side.
2. **Time Your Jumps**: Practice timing your jumps to avoid obstacles and gaps. Jumping too early or too late can lead to mistakes.
