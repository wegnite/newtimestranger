## Basic Controls and Interface

Mastering the controls is the first step to becoming a successful thief. Here’s a quick breakdown:

### Movement
- **Touch and Drag**: To move your character, simply touch the screen and drag your finger in the desired direction. Your character will follow your movements seamlessly.

### Interacting with Objects
- **Tap to Interact**: Need to open a door or flip a switch? Just tap on the object to interact with it.

### Picking Up Items
- **Automatic Pickup**: When you walk over items like keys or tools, your character will automatically pick them up. No extra steps required!

### Using Items
- **Inventory Selection**: Collected items appear in your inventory at the bottom of the screen. Tap on an item to select it, then tap on the object in the game world where you want to use it.
