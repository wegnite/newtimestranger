## Basic Controls and Interface

Mastering the controls is key to success in Stickman Clash. Here’s a quick breakdown:

- **Move**: Use the WASD keys to move your stickman around the arena.
- **Attack**: Left-click to attack with your weapon.
- **Jump**: Press the Spacebar to jump and avoid attacks.
- **Pick Up/Use Item**: Use the E key to pick up items or interact with objects.
- **Switch Weapons**: If you have multiple weapons, press Q to switch between them.

The interface is simple and intuitive. Your health bar is displayed at the top of the screen, and your weapon is shown in the bottom corner. Keep an eye on both to stay in the fight!
