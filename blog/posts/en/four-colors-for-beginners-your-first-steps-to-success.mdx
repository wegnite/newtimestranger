## What is Four Colors?

**Four Colors** is a free, browser-based card game that brings the thrill of UNO to your screen. The goal is simple: be the first player to get rid of all your cards. With its vibrant colors, strategic gameplay, and multiplayer options, it's a fantastic way to challenge friends or meet new opponents from around the world.

The game features 108 cards divided into four colors, each with numbers and special action cards. Whether you're playing with 2, 3, or 4 players, **Four Colors** offers endless entertainment and opportunities to outsmart your opponents.

## Basic Controls and Interface

**Four Colors** is designed to be intuitive. Here’s a quick rundown of the controls and interface:

- **Left Mouse Button**: Use this to select and play cards.  
- **"1" Button**: Press this when you have only one card left to avoid penalties.  
- **Draw Pile**: Click here to draw a card if you don’t have a playable option.  
- **Discard Pile**: This is where the last played card is displayed.  

The interface is clean and user-friendly, so you can focus on the game without distractions.

## First Game Walkthrough

Let’s walk through your first game step by step:

1. **Start the Game**: After selecting your mode, you’ll be dealt 7 cards.  
2. **Match the Top Card**: The game begins with a card in the discard pile. Play a card that matches its color or number.  
3. **Use Action Cards**: If you have a Skip, Reverse, or Draw Two card, use it strategically to disrupt your opponents.  
4. **Wild Cards**: Play a Wild or Wild Draw 4 card to change the color and gain an advantage.  
5. **Call "1"**: When you’re down to your last card, press the "1" button to avoid penalties.  
6. **Win the Game**: Be the first to play all your cards and claim victory!  

### Pro Tip:  
Always keep an eye on your opponents’ card counts. If someone has only a few cards left, try to force them to draw more with action cards.

## Next Steps

Once you’ve mastered the basics, it’s time to level up your gameplay. Here are some advanced strategies to consider:

1. **Bluff with Wild Draw 4**: Use this card wisely to surprise your opponents.  
2. **Save Action Cards**: Don’t use your Skip or Reverse cards too early. Save them for critical moments.  
3. **Plan Ahead**: Think about your next moves and anticipate your opponents’ strategies.  
4. **Play Multiplayer**: Challenge friends or join global matches to test your skills against different play styles.
