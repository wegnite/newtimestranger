This is a walkthrough for the The Time Stranger mission in Digimon Story Time Stranger. Read on to see how to complete all of its objectives and progress to the next Main Mission!

## The Time Stranger Walkthrough

### The Time Stranger Guide

|  | Mission Guide |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | ![The Time Stranger Step 1](/images/walkthorough/d341f692d59ef992.webp) After waking up, go down to the living room and speak with Inori. |
| 2 | ![The Time Stranger Step 2](/images/walkthorough/d3910fe29ef9c768.webp) Head over to the office of Inori's dad. Upon exiting the Misono Home, you just need to go down a few steps of stairs to find the entrance to the office on your right. |
| 3 | ![The Time Stranger Step 3](/images/walkthorough/3884d657df2c5abb.webp) After speaking to Kosuke Misono, follow the quest marker and go to Shinjuku Station: Jubancho Intersection. Take the stairs down to the underground station and head to the marked location. |
| 4 | ![The Time Stranger Step 4](/images/walkthorough/7cff1e54546ab51a.webp) Leave the underground station and follow the quest marker all the way to the southeastern Taxi Stand area in Seibu-Shinjuku: Overbridge Intersection. |
| 5 | ![The Time Stranger Step 5](/images/walkthorough/b04b2c34c9d4c367.webp) Continue following the quest marker to reach the marked location in Shinjuku Park: Waterfall Plaza. |
| 6 | ![The Time Stranger Step 6](/images/walkthorough/34561f2fcbc59e91.webp) Go to Shinjuku: A Certain Back Alley and look for the bright light in the highlighted location. Interact with it to enter the In-Between Theater. |
| 7 | ![The Time Stranger Step 7](/images/walkthorough/0101c474f7f7450b.webp) Speak with the purple-haired receptionist at the west side of the area. You'll unlock the Digifarm, which allows you to passively farm EXP and Materials.▶︎ [Digifarm Guide](/games/Digimon-Story-Time-Stranger/archives/554989) |
| 8 | ![The Time Stranger Step 8](/images/walkthorough/dcd674c7418948e4.webp) After leaving the In-Between Theater, you'll be prompted to return to the attic at the Misono Household. Check out the clothes rack to change your Costume and end the day!▶︎ [List of Costumes](/games/Digimon-Story-Time-Stranger/archives/554926) |
