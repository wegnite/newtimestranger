## The Beginning of the End Walkthrough Strategy

|  | The Beginning of the End Guide |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | ![Nishi-Shinjuku](/images/walkthorough/b63e7409f65d1f6f.webp) At Nishi-Shinjuku: Vision Square, head to the objective marker leading to an alley. The city will be rife with hostile Digimon, so expect to battle some on your way. |
| 2 | ![Go Toward Blue Barriers](/images/walkthorough/d3dab13f604b6f07.webp) Past the alley, go straight until you see armed forces by blue barriers, triggering a cutscene. |
| 3 | ![Go into Subway](/images/walkthorough/524fc1983f0a1108.webp) After the cutscene, head underground through the subway. |
| 4 | ![Pass Through Doors](/images/walkthorough/38b6908df43abd65.webp) Go to the objective marker at the end of the station past the doors. A cutscene will play, after which BlackGatomon will join your team for the next mission. |
