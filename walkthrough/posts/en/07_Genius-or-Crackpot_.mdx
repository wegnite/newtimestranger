## Genius or Crackpot? Walkthrough Strategy

### Complete Walkthrough Guide

|  | Mission Guide |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | ![Misono Home](/images/walkthorough/78640da16eb2b02d.webp) Head down to the living room and talk to Kosuke and Aegiomon. During the conversation, Kosuke will ask you to come with him to meet someone. |
| 2 | ![Shinjuku Park](/images/walkthorough/b53ed1cecdf86e7c.webp) Once in Shinjuku Park, head toward the marker to meet Chief Investigator Kodai Kuremi and the scientist Monica Simmons. Dr. Simmons will then begin an experiment involving Aegiomon. |
| 3 | ![Aegiomon battle experiment](/images/walkthorough/14acdf6a75c6b42e.webp) For the experiment, Aegiomon will be tasked with attacking a soldier holding a shield. Keep using the Wind Claw skill to continue the test. There’s no danger here, as the soldier will not attack. |
| 4 | ![Pursue Aegiomon and the children](/images/walkthorough/b6ee5bc60db6a59a.webp) Aegiomon will spend some time playing with the children in the park. Follow the marker to find them. Once the event is over, you’ll return to Misono Home. The mission ends after a conversation with the operator. |
